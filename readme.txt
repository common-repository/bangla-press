Bangla Press Pro is a versatile WordPress plugin that displays news from any newspapers or websites using RSS feeds on your site.

== Description ==

**Bangla Press Pro** is a powerful plugin that displays news titles, thumbnails, and summaries from RSS feeds.It offers flexibility in customizing the number of news items, whether to show photos, and the summary word count. You can integrate news feeds into posts, pages, or widgets with ease.

== Key Features ==


-  **Display News Titles, Thumbnails, and Summaries**: Show comprehensive news content from RSS feeds.
-  **Customize the Number of Items Displayed**: Control how many news items you want to show.
-  **Option to Show or Hide Photos**: Choose whether to include thumbnails with your news items.
-  **Set the Word Count for Summaries**: Adjust the length of news summaries to fit your needs.
-  **Integrate News Feeds via Shortcode or Widget**: Easily add news feeds to posts, pages, or widgets.
-  **Display News Title or Full News**: Use shortcodes to show either news titles or full news items anywhere on your site.
-  **Dedicated Widget for News Titles**: Add a widget that displays only news titles.
-  **Add RSS Feeds from Various Sources**: Easily integrate RSS feeds from a range of newspapers and websites.
- ️ **Easy Setup, Instant News Updates**: No tech skills required! With Bangla Press Pro.
-  **Display News Anywhere with a Simple Shortcode**: Bring dynamic news updates to your site with Bangla Press Pro's widget feature.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `news-feed-pro` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Configure the plugin settings by navigating to the 'Bangla Press Pro' menu in the WordPress admin dashboard.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I use the shortcode? =

To display news with photos and summery, use the following shortcode:

[rss_news_with_photos feed_url="https://www.nbcnews.com/feed" num_items="5" show_photos="true" word_count="20"]


To display only news titles, use:

[rss_titles feed_url="https://feeds.feedburner.com/NDTV-LatestNews" num_items="5"]


You can customize the `feed_url`, `num_items`, `show_photos`, and `word_count` attributes to match your preferences.

= How do I insert news feeds into widgets? =

To display news in widgets, follow these options:
- **Title Only Widget**: Title Only - Display News Title Only.
- **News With Photos and Summary Widget**: News With Photos and Summary - Display News With Photo and Summary.
